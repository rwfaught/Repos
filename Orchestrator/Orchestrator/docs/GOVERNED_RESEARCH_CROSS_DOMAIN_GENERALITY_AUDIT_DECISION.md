# Governed Research Cross-Domain Generality Audit Decision

## Status and Authority

`DOCUMENT_CLASS=DURABLE_CTO_ARCHITECTURE_AUDIT_DECISION`

`AUDIT_CROSS_DOMAIN_GENERALITY=COMPLETED_CTO_RATIFIED`

`GOVERNED_RESEARCH_V1_DESIGN_GATE=READY_WITH_NAMED_PRECONDITIONS`

This CTO-ratified decision governs architectural interpretation and the next
design gate. It is not implementation proof, does not authorize design or
implementation by itself, and does not select a public proof domain.

## Executive Conclusion

A narrow reusable core exists, but it is smaller than the dossier-shaped
implementation boundary. Existing dossier structures must not be generalized
wholesale. Governed Research V1 should use selective semantic reuse through a
research application adapter.

## Accepted Neutral Semantics

`NEUTRAL_EVIDENCE_LINK=GENUINELY_NEUTRAL`

`DETERMINISTIC_DIAGNOSTIC_BLOCKING=GENUINELY_NEUTRAL`

`RECOMMENDATION_HUMAN_DISPOSITION_SEPARATION=GENUINELY_NEUTRAL`

`EXPLICIT_ENTRY_IDENTITY_AND_CALLER_DECLARED_TRANSITIONS=NEUTRAL_WITH_CONSTRAINTS`

`CONTRADICTION_AND_MISSING_INFORMATION_VISIBILITY=NEUTRAL_WITH_CONSTRAINTS`

These classifications concern preserved semantics, not proof of general
research competence.

## Adapter-Mediated Reuse

`CASE_PACKET_PERSISTENCE=REUSABLE_VIA_RESEARCH_APPLICATION_ADAPTER`

`PACKET_LOCAL_SYNTHESIS_PRESENTATION=REUSABLE_VIA_RESEARCH_APPLICATION_ADAPTER`

Persistence behavior and protected identity collections may be useful, but the
current schema is not research-neutral. The adapter must remain in the research
application layer; it may not merely rename semantically mismatched fields.

## Application-Layer Exclusions

`CONTROLLED_DOSSIER_WORKFLOW=APPLICATION_LAYER_ONLY`

`CURRENT_CASE_PACKET_SCHEMA=NOT_NEUTRAL_WHOLESALE`

`CANDIDATE_PRIORITIZATION_RECOMMENDATION_PACKET=NOT_RESEARCH_CANONICAL`

The dossier-shaped assumptions excluded from the neutral core include
counterparties, timeline events, drafts, decisions, dossier readiness,
candidate comparison, prioritization judgment, and operational recommendation
assumptions.

## Provenance Implications

`PHASE_5_PROVENANCE=INCOMPLETE`

`GENERALIZED_PHASE_6=INCOMPLETE`

Current evidence does not justify claims of source completeness, collection
reproducibility, source quality, claim lineage, extraction correctness,
freshness, or evidentiary sufficiency. A later narrow proof may use
caller-supplied stable source IDs, source locators, claims/statements, and
explicit asserted associations without claiming complete provenance.

## Neutral-Core Admission Rule

A research-derived abstraction may enter the neutral core only when it:

1. preserves the same semantic invariant across dossier and research;
2. has evidence from two materially different workflows;
3. preserves provenance, uncertainty, authorization, and disposition meaning;
4. requires no domain vocabulary in the core contract;
5. introduces no duplicate convenience path or speculative framework machinery;
6. has an intelligible bounded failure mode; and
7. is justified by concrete repeated cross-workflow burden.

This is a CTO-ratified design constraint, not proof that any new abstraction
currently qualifies.

## Governed Research V1 Design Preconditions

`GOVERNED_RESEARCH_V1_DESIGN_GATE=READY_WITH_NAMED_PRECONDITIONS`

Before design may proceed, it must define a research application adapter rather
than generalizing `case_packet`; define research-specific source-plan,
collection, source-evaluation, and claim-extraction boundaries; specify minimum
provenance and claim lineage; preserve “no conclusion” and “no recommendation”
outcomes; name the exact invariant preserved by every reused structure; and
keep collection, evaluation, extraction, and analytical judgment outside claims
about current implementation.

## Advisory Proof-Domain Candidates

The following are advisory and not selected:

1. public historical-record claim brief;
2. public standards/guidance comparison; and
3. small public scientific-literature evidence note.

`PUBLIC_SOURCE_PROOF_DOMAIN=NOT_YET_SELECTED`

The historical-record claim brief is the current leading candidate, not a
ratified selection.

## Next Governed Boundary

The next candidate boundary is `DESIGN_GOVERNED_RESEARCH_V1`. Its design must
remain bounded and non-implementation. Public-source proof remains blocked
pending design and separate authorization; research implementation remains
unauthorized.

## Explicit Non-Proofs

This decision does not prove cross-domain runtime competence, complete
provenance, research collection, source evaluation, claim extraction,
contradiction discovery, analytical correctness, recommendation correctness,
public-source proof success, a selected proof domain, a product wedge,
provider/model suitability, implementation readiness, or production readiness.
