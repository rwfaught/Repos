## Decision Context

The Small-Business AI Implementation Dossier has produced meaningful
fictional and structural evidence. It has not produced real participant or
real engagement evidence. Current engagement progress depends on an actual
participant, which is presently an external availability constraint.

Continuing dossier-shaped expansion without a participant risks producing more
domain elaboration than platform evidence. Before further domain-specific
momentum is allowed to shape the core, the project needs evidence about
cross-domain generality. This is a resequencing decision, not a rejection of
the dossier or its accepted work.
